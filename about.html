About Us

Serenti Africa

Serenti Africa is a 360 degree service provider within the real estate and interior design space. At Serenti, we hold your hand and guide you through the process of property acquisition, interior design and décor and property management. With us, you do not have to lift a finger, just watch your dreams take up shape just as you envisioned it.

Message from the Founder

I have always had a thing for elegant spaces, this love for gorgeous spaces had me poring over magazines and viewing beautiful homes and spaces ever since I was a little girl. The defining moment came in my final year, while pursuing my undergraduate studies, this was in 2013, I had a room to myself and did it up to my taste with the meager student budget that I had.

I was a student leader at the time and some meetings would happen in my room, I got many compliments about my room, the textures, the colors etc. This pointed me to the fact that maybe, just maybe there was something there.

Right after school in 2014, I registered Serenti as a business with the encouragement and help of some angels in my life and that is how Serenti was birthed.

Over the years, serenti has catered to clients from all walks of life and done many projects; fit outs for offices, homes, kitchen designs etc.

A thing that is meant to be a giant will forever go through many rebirths, as Serenti inches towards the decade mark, we have finally found our pulse; to go back to right where the love started; the love for luxury spaces that have personality and speak of the owners’ and users’ identity and that is what we set out to do for the next decade; design bespoke spaces that ooze personality while capturing a luxurious essence.

Serenti has always been a “God Idea” and every new development, success and progress is attributable to God. As God would have it, we are happy to present to you the next chapter of Serenti; Serenti Redefined, stick with us for this exhilarating ride.
